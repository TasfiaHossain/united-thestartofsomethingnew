“Hope is important because it can make the present moment less difficult to bear. If we believe that tomorrow will be better, we can bear a hardship today.” - Thich Nhat Hanh

2020 has been a chaotic year. With a pandemic that has hit us with dire conditions in our society, our economy, and our health. People all over NYC, once known as the epi-center for COVID-19, have been struggling to keep up with what this pandemic has brought us; unemployment, rising food prices, pressure on paying rent, and horrible medical diagnosis. With this website, we wanted to create a resource that would allow people struggling with those mattes to find places that would help provide for them during this time of need. We created a website that can easily be navigated upon, providing links, websites, alerts, and features that can be accessed to virtually anyone. We hope this can help everyone!

UNITED

Based on the interviews we have conducted, the community has agreed that resources such as food banks, government programs, and medical centers for COVID-19 are major issues in 2020. We want to build an organization that will show information on these problems and can provide those in need of help.
